# 1. Vision

To become India's most trusted AI-powered railway reservation intelligence platform, helping every passenger make smarter travel decisions through accurate predictions, booking intelligence, and data-driven insights.

# 2. Mission

To empower railway passengers with intelligent reservation insights by providing AI-powered confirmation predictions, booking strategy recommendations, quota analysis, and transparent explanations that help them make informed travel decisions.

# 3. Problem Statement

Indian railway passengers often face uncertainty while booking reserved tickets. Existing platforms primarily display booking status or a confirmation probability but provide limited explanation of the factors affecting the prediction. Passengers frequently struggle to choose the best train, travel date, class, booking station, or quota to maximize their chances of ticket confirmation.

RailSense addresses this problem by combining AI-powered prediction, railway reservation intelligence, quota analysis, booking strategy optimization, and transparent explanations to help passengers make informed travel decisions with greater confidence.

# 5. Product Goals

RailSense aims to:

- Improve passengers' confidence while booking railway tickets.
- Provide accurate and explainable ticket confirmation predictions.
- Recommend the best booking strategy based on available reservation data.
- Simplify complex railway reservation concepts such as GNWL, RLWL, PQWL, and TQWL.
- Help users choose the best train, class, date, and booking station.
- Continuously improve prediction accuracy using historical and community-contributed data.
- Build a trusted AI-powered railway reservation intelligence platform.

# 6. Core Features (MVP)

## 6.1 AI Ticket Confirmation Prediction
- Predict confirmation probability for WL/RAC tickets.
- Display confidence level and prediction explanation.

## 6.2 Booking Strategy Optimizer
- Recommend the best booking station.
- Suggest the best boarding station.
- Compare alternative booking strategies.

## 6.3 Quota Explorer
- Explain GNWL, RLWL, PQWL, TQWL and other reservation quotas.
- Display quota boundaries and charting stations.
- Help users understand why a specific quota is assigned.

## 6.4 Smart Train Recommendation
- Recommend the best train based on confirmation chances.
- Compare available classes.
- Suggest alternate travel dates.

## 6.5 Waitlist Trend Analysis
- Show historical waitlist movement.
- Identify increasing or decreasing demand.

## 6.6 Explainable AI
- Explain every prediction.
- Show factors influencing the prediction.
- Display prediction confidence.

# 9. MVP Scope (Version 1.0)

The first public version of RailSense will include:

- AI Ticket Confirmation Prediction
- Booking Strategy Optimizer
- Quota Explorer
- Smart Train Recommendation
- Waitlist Trend Analysis
- Explainable AI

The following features are NOT included in Version 1.0:

- User accounts and profiles
- Community data contribution
- Mobile applications
- AI chatbot
- Multi-language support
- Premium subscription
- Offline mode

# 10. Product Principles

RailSense will always follow these principles:

- Accuracy over guesswork.
- Explain every prediction.
- Keep the user interface simple.
- Use data to support recommendations.
- Be transparent about prediction confidence.
- Respect user privacy.
- Continuously improve using real-world feedback.
